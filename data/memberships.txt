NUST Student & Alumni Discount:
- Current NUST students get 30% off all membership plans.
- NUST alumni get 20% off all membership plans.

How to Avail NUST Discount:
1. For students: Present a valid, active NUST Student ID at the reception.
2. For alumni: Present a valid NUST Alumni ID or certificate at the reception.
3. The discount is applied at the time of registration.
4. Discounts are non-transferable and applicable only to the student/alumni themselves.
5. The student or alumni may be asked to verify enrollment or graduation status through university records.
Note:
Discounts cannot be shared with family members or friends. Only one discount is applicable per person.
